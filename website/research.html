Research agenda

Safety-constrained self-driving storage.

ArgosFS positions autonomy as something measurable: decisions, safety checks, verification, and artifacts all become data.

Observe

SMART fields, latency EWMAs, capacity provenance, boot-critical classification, and workload heat.

Diagnose

Risk memory confirms unhealthy disks and separates stale measurements from fresh observations.

Plan

Dry-run explain records expose chosen action, rejected actions, safety checks, and expected utility.

Verify

Mutation runs end with fsck verification and adaptive mode downgrade on failure.

Artifact evaluation

One command to produce reviewable artifacts.

The experiment framework writes raw JSONL/CSV, processed summaries, figures, tables, and a manifest with kernel, commit, mode, and seed.

scripts/experiments/run_all.sh --quick --output paper-data/runs/ae-quick
python3 scripts/experiments/summarize_results.py paper-data/runs/ae-quick/raw paper-data/runs/ae-quick

Experiment families

From smoke tests to paper figures.

Failure matrix

Clean writes, interrupted writes, rename recovery, degraded reads, and self-heal paths.

Workload shifts

Hot/cold phase changes measure placement convergence and background interference.

QEMU rootfs

Boot and degraded-mode records for normal, interrupted, and emergency-mode scenarios.

Baselines
